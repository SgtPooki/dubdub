Top 5 Reasons to Consider the Mozu Cloud Commerce Platform

< View All Resources

Mozu powers the rich digital brand experiences that engage shoppers on their terms, on any device, and drive more sales today. Whether you’re a retailer looking to free yourself from the hassles of managing a homegrown system, evolve beyond the limitations of a legacy platform, or solve the complexities of omni-channel shopping, it’s time for you to meet Mozu.

1. Connected Digital Experiences

The Mozu platform enables users to build personalized, convenient shopping experiences that engage and convert more shoppers – and at a site speed 4x faster than most platforms. With responsive design native to the platform, your site experience will render perfectly on any device.

Did you know 85% of consumers
expect a seamless mobile experience?

2. Empowered Marketers

Marketers now have complete control of the digital experience, saving your business time and resources. Mozu’s intuitive usability and single master catalog create efficiencies and streamline the operational workflows of inventory management and fulfillment, and enable marketing teams to launch sites, accelerating time-to-market.

“Migrating from our homegrown platform to Mozu has given Jelly Belly the power to drive growth much faster than we could on our own.”
Brandon Finch, Director of eBusinss, Jelly Belly

3. Open API

With our API-first approach, customers and partners have full access to the same API used to create the Mozu platform. Developers can scale and control every aspect of the Mozu platform - from themes to custom integration to admin functionality.

4. Open Source SDKs

Java? PHP? .NET? Mozu offers Open Source SDKs for these languages and many more, empowering developers to quickly and cost-effectively deploy the technologies of your choice using the languages of their choice. Businesses can easily create functionality, add custom features and extend the capability of Mozu's robust theming engine on their own terms.

5. True Multi-Tenant SaaS

Mozu’s SaaS platform delivers rapid deployment, no hardware headaches, easy maintenance, and regular release of new features that will never catch you by surprise or disrupt your business. We focus on availability so you can focus on the business at hand. True multi-tenant SaaS ensures Mozu customers with optimal performance, increased security and failover rates – even during seasonal commerce peaks – resulting in unparalleled uptimes and content delivery speeds.

“We are laser-focused on delivering an experience that excites and engages our customers. We are blazing a trail to implement best-in-breed technologies and Mozu is key to helping us power that innovation.”
Tony Barnes, Chief Technology Officer, Bluefly
