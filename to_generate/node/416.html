  • Mozu Partners with eBridge Connections for ERP Integration Solutions

    Partnership reflects commitment to provide best-in-class integrated omni-channel commerce solutions to the Mozu developer and merchant communities

    Tuesday Apr 21, 2015
  • AUSTIN, TX and BURLINGTON, ON (April 21, 2015) Mozu, the cloud commerce platform built by Volusion, today announced its partnership with eBridge Connections, the leader in accounting and ERP integration solutions for eCommerce. The partnership is designed to further simplify and support the needs of Mozu’s growing list of mid-market to enterprise-level clients by enabling scalable data integration between the Mozu platform and a selection of industry-leading ERP systems.

    “With the development of this application we are able to facilitate the delivery of scalable and customer-friendly integration solutions, and are positioned to strengthen Mozu’s mantra of Limitless Commerce,” said Neil Patel, Director of Business Development at Mozu.

    The Mozu connector developed by eBridge Connections enables universal accounting and ERP integration with products from Microsoft, SAP, Sage, NetSuite, Epicor, and others. More specifically, this connector will provide Mozu clients with bi-directional, automated integration of sales orders and inventory across systems, with other data touchpoints in development.

    “Mozu’s robust API has allowed us to build a deep and flexible integration connector with the advanced data touchpoints that enterprise commerce merchants demand,” said Colin Brown, CEO at eBridge Connections. “With the development of this connector we are well-equipped to deliver scalable and customer-friendly integration solutions to the Mozu community.”

    About Mozu
    Mozu is the commerce platform for today’s omni-channel marketplace. With Mozu, retailers can manage their commerce, content and customer experience across every channel and on any platform. Designed with an API-first architecture and built to meet the demands of the modern consumer, Mozu provides truly limitless commerce to an ever-changing business landscape. To learn more, visit mozu.com and @MozuCommerce.

    About eBridge Connections
    eBridge Connections delivers powerful, cloud-based accounting integration solutions that automate vital business processes and eliminate the need for manual data entry. The eBridge universal integration platform supports over 20 accounting and ERP systems including products from Microsoft, Sage, SAP, NetSuite, and Epicor. With connections to the most popular eCommerce carts and marketplaces, hundreds of EDI trading partners and the leading CRM systems, eBridge Connections has become the integration platform of choice for businesses worldwide. For more information, please visit www.ebridgeconnections.com and @eBridgeConnects.
